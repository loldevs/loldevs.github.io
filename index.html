Welcome to our LoL development community.

About Who? What? Why?

We are a bunch of developers, who have been brought together by enthusiasm in open-source development, thirst for knowledge and most importantly love for League. We started of in February 2014 with the goal to improve the experience of League players by exploring how League works, sharing the knowledge we gather and making use of it in a variety of software solutions.

Projects

Here is a (incomplete) list projects we are working on.

  • leaguespec

    The main collection of knowledge we have gathered.

  • lol-observer Javascript

    A piece of software written to parse data from the spectator stream of League.

  • lol-spec Java

    Another project with the aim of studying the League spectator stream

We also encourage you to check out these cool League related projects.

  • LogsOfLag

    A League of Legends netlog analyzer

  • Riftwalk.gg

    Incredibly detailed game statistics

  • LoL Nexus

    Realtime game search

If you know of a project that you think we should include here, or are working on one yourself, let us know! You can find the details on how to get in touch with us down in the contribution section.

Contributing

Fresh blood is always welcome with loldevs! Most of us don't have too much time to work on pet-projects, so any help would be greatly appreciated. Some of the things you can help us with include

  • writing or cleaning up documentation,
  • writing guides and tutorials,
  • doing research,
  • developing reference implementations.

The best way to get in touch with us is to join our IRC channel, which is ##loldev@chat.freenode.net (yes, with 2 hashtags).
